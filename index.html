About Us

At Rosella, flowers are more than just petals — they're a language of emotion. Our mission is to deliver beauty, elegance, and personalized floral arrangements that tell your story. Whether it's a wedding, birthday, or just a heartfelt gift, we believe every bloom should leave a memory.

Our History

Founded in 2020 by three flower enthusiasts, Rosella blossomed from a small idea into a full-service floral experience. At first, we started delivering only to the West Bank, then gradually expanded to cover all areas across Palestine. We’ve worked with hundreds of clients, turning their events into dreamy, vibrant moments full of life and color. We continue to grow with love, creativity, and a commitment to premium quality.

Why Choose Rosella?

  • Same-day delivery with love and care
  • Unique arrangements tailored to your occasions
